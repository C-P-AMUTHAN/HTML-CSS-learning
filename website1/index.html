Cyclone Michaung | Tamil Nadu CM Stalin announces ₹6,000 as relief amount to all flood-affected families

Tamil Nadu Chief Minister M.K. Stalin on Saturday, December 9, 2023, announced ₹6,000 each, to all of the families that have been affected by Cyclone Michaung. The relief amount will be distributed through fair price shops in all affected areas. The Chief Minister, who chaired a meeting attended by his Cabinet colleagues, the T.N. Chief Secretary and other officials, also increased the solatium to ₹5 lakh from ₹4 lakh for those who lost their lives in the floods.

The flood waters swirling and running through residential areas of Ennore and Eranavur have a thick film of oil covering it. The oil is forming a layer on the walls, the objects it touches inside flooded homes and people crossing the water to get out of their homes. A video made by two volunteers of Vijay Makkal Iyakkam crossing the oil-layered waters while carrying relief to residents went viral on social media on Thursday. Vicky, one of the volunteers, told The Hindu that he and his friend Kishore had to wade through the flood water since the residents had contacted them for assistance and had asked for water. “We had to scrub several times to remove the oil from our body. The clothes had to be discarded, as they could not be salvaged. I can still smell the oil,” he said.

India vs South Africa T20 series 2023

Fresh off a 4-1 series triumph at home, the Indian men’s cricket team will be on the road to face South Africa in a three-match T20 series, starting Sunday. Durban’s Kingsmead will host the first India vs South Africa T20 cricket match. St George's Park in Gqeberha will be the venue for the second tie while the final match will be at the Wanderers in Johannesburg. Live streaming and telecast of all three IND vs SA T20 matches will be available in India.

Suryakumar Yadav will continue as India’s cricket captain in the T20 series against the Proteas after leading the team to a comfortable series win against Australia at home. It was Suryakumar Yadav’s first series as India’s T20 captain. For the tour to South Africa, the Indian cricket team will be bolstered by the addition of Ravindra Jadeja, who will be serving as the vice-captain for the T20 series, batting star Shubman Gill and ace pacer Mohammed Siraj. All three were rested for the Australia series after their involvement in the ODI World Cup. South Africa fast bowler Lungi Ngidi, meanwhile, will skip the T20 series due to injury. The 27-year-old pacer is recovering from an ankle sprain.
The T20 series will be followed by a three-match ODI series, for which KL Rahul has been handed the responsibility of captaining the Indian squad. India’s tour of South Africa concludes with two Test matches, for which captain Rohit Sharma and Virat Kohli will join the squad.

38 US states affected by cantaloupe-related salmonella outbreak, 3 dead

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) has warned people against the consumption of Malchita or Rudy brand cantaloupes.

Salmonella outbreak in the United States and Canada has claimed the lives of at least eight persons. Health agencies believe that cantaloupes are the source of salmonella infections which has created a health crisis in both countries. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) has warned people against the consumption of Malchita or Rudy brand cantaloupes. Do not eat pre-cut cantaloupes if you don’t know whether Malchita or Rudy brand cantaloupes were used , the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention said on its website. "Do not eat any recalled whole or pre-cut cantaloupe products." According to CDC, in the United States, 230 persons are sick in 38 states, with 96 hospitalised and 3 deaths. According to the Public Health Agency of Canada, there have been five deaths due to cantaloupe-related salmonella outbreak in the country.
